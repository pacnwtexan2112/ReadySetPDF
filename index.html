The Full Prepress Arsenal

ReadySetPDF doesn't just view files; it engineers them for the press. Explore the complete suite of tools included in version 2.19.

Step & Repeat Imposition Core Tool

Maximize your sheet yield instantly. Input your custom row and column counts, define exact gutter spacing, and let the engine step-and-repeat single records into production-ready multi-up press sheets in seconds. Ideal for business cards, postcards, and tags.

Automated Booklet Maker Core Tool

Stop wrestling with reader-to-printer spreads manually. ReadySetPDF automatically paginates multipage documents into perfect 2-up saddle stitch layouts. It calculates and applies precise spine creep based on your specific paper caliper, ensuring perfectly flush face trims every time.

Crop & Bleed Mark Generator

Generating printer marks shouldn't be a guessing game. Apply professional perimeter crop marks, center marks, and define trim boxes globally across any document. Ensure your bindery department has exactly the edge-to-edge indicators they need for perfect guillotine cuts.

VDP & CSV Mail Merge

Process Variable Data Printing streams rapidly. Easily link CSV data sets to your PDF templates to generate personalized direct mailers, healthcare compliance letters, or promotional items. Automatically formats address blocks and variable text for production machinery.

Batch File Merger

Consolidate chaos into a single print run. Seamlessly merge dozens or hundreds of individual PDF files into one continuous, optimized master document. Keeps formatting intact and drastically reduces RIP time on your digital presses.

Page Extraction & Splitting

Break down massive batch files into manageable chunks for different presses. Extract specific page ranges, split a 10,000-page file into 1,000-page segments, or pull single records out of a corrupted print stream without having to go back to the original designer.

Automated Blank Page Insertion

Fix duplexing errors before they hit the press. Automatically insert blank pages to force right-hand reading, pad documents to divisible page counts for booklet making, or ensure chapter starts always land on the front of a sheet.

Document Resizing & Scaling

Standardize customer files instantly. Force non-standard client PDFs into standard print dimensions (Letter, Tabloid, Custom) while intelligently maintaining aspect ratios, centering content, and automatically adjusting the trim box definitions.
